7th Grade Science

Curriculum

7th grade science in the state of Texas focuses primarily on life science. We will cover 9 units throughout this school year.

  • Scientific Method
  • Cell Structures and Functions
  • Human Body Systems
  • Plant Systems
  • Reproduction and Heredity
  • Adaptations and Biodiversity
  • Living Systems: Ecology
  • Earth's Changing Surface
  • Humans in Space

I have been teaching this curriculum for the past five years and love to find new and creative ways to guide students through it!
